About Me.

A California native; Annie was forged by the tempestuous waters of an East Coast art education at RISD. She has an absurd love of: tiny “Kauai” things, thought-provoking books & her Ewok-dog named Honeybean. What was being a printmaker at Gemini G.E.L. like? Maybe she will tell you someday. For now, she is giving her standard 110%, to the tantalizing ether of UCI’s UX/UI bootcamp & loving it.

It is always the right time to make the most of your best ideas.
